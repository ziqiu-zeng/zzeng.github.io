Ziqiu ZENG

I am a Research Fellow at the Human-Centered Robotic Lab, National University of Singapore, where I work on Computer Graphics (Physics-Based Simulations), Robotic Learning & Control, and High-Performance Computing (HPC). My research develops efficient computational methods to bridge virtual simulations and physical robotic systems. I received my Ph.D. from the University of Strasbourg, where I was part of the INRIA Mimesis team, specializing in real-time physics simulation and numerical methods for dynamic systems. My current work focuses on integrating physics-based dynamics with learning algorithms to create robust and scalable robotic solutions.
